		<h2 id = "a"> Incendiary </h2>
		<p> Brimstone's first ability is Incendiary. Press Q to equip an incendiary grenade launcher. Fire (left-click) to launch a grenade that detonates after it comes to a rest on the floor, creating a fire zone that damages players heavily within the zone. </p>

		<h2 id = "b"> Sky Smoke </h2>
		<p> Brimstone's second ability is Sky Smoke. Press Q to equip a tactical map. Fire (left-click) to set locations where the smoke clouds will land. Alternate Fire (right-click) to launch the smoke clouds that block vision in the selected area.</p>

		<h2 id = "c"> Stim Beacon </h2>
		<p> Brimstone's third ability is Stim Beacon. Press C to toss the stim beacon in front of Brimstone. Upon landing, the stim beacon will create a field that enhances teammates. </p>

		<h2 id = "d"> Orbital Strike </h2>
		<p> Brimstone's fourth ability also known as the ultimate is Orbital Strike. Press X to equip a tactical map. Fire (left-click) to launch a orbital strike laser at the selected location, dealing enough damage to kill a player if they stay in the orbital strike. It lasts about 5 seconds </p>
