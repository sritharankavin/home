Role

Reyna's role is Duelist.

Abilities

Reyna has 4 abilities that can be used to blind enemies and help her team get onto site. Most of her abilities is kill-based, so if you don't get a kill, then the ability is useless, but because of that, a lot of people like to play Reyna because of her selfish abilities which heals her and lets her get away from fights.

Leer

Press C to equip an eye that takes away vision from enemies. Press C to throw the eye at a short distance wherever the crosshair is aimed at. The eye will Nearsight all enemies who look at it. The eye can be destroyed by enemies.

Dismiss

Enemies killed by Reyna leave behind Soul Orbs that last 3 seconds. Press E to consume a nearby soul orb of an fallen enemy that Reyna has killed, making her intangible for a short amount of time. If Empress is active, Reyna will become invisible when she uses Dismiss.

This video is outdated and instead of 4 uses in total, Reyna can use Dismiss and Devour 2 times in total.

Devour

Enemies killed by Reyna leave behind Soul Orbs that last 3 seconds. Press Q to consume a nearby ssoul orb, healing Reyna for a short duration. Health gained through this ability that exceed 100 will decay over time. If Empress is active, the skill will automatically cast and not consume an orb.

This video is outdated and instead of 4 uses in total, Reyna can use Dismiss and Devour 2 times in total.

Empress

Press X to enter a frenzy, enhancing firing speed, equip and reload speed. Killing an enemy renews the duration of this frenzy.
