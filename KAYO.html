Role

KAY/O's role is Initiator.

Abilities

KAY/O is a machine of war built for a single purpose which is neutralizing radiants, so most of his abilities are used to suppress enemies.

Ability Default Key Uses/Restock Credit/Points
Frag/Ment C 1 200
Flash/Drive Q 2 250
Zero/Point E 1 (Use) 40 seconds (Restock) Free
Null/CMD X N/A 7 Ultimate Points

Flash/Drive

Press Q to equip a flash grenade. Fire (left-click) to throw the flash grenade which explodes after a set amount of time, blinding anyone in the line of sight. Alternate fire (right-click) to toss the flash grenade.


Zero/Point

Press E to equip a supression knife. Fire (left-click) to throw the suppression knife at the direction of your crosshair. When on contact with a surface, the knife sticks, winds up, and supressus anyone in the radius of the explosion.


Frag/Ment

Press C to equip an explosive grenade. Fire (left-click) to throw the grenade. When on contact with the ground, the grenade sticks to the ground and explodes multiple time, dealing high damage at the center with each explosion.


Null/CMD

Press X to instantly overload with radianite energy that empowers KAY/O and causes large energy pulses to emit from his location. Enemies in the range of these pulses are suppresed for a short amount of time if they get out of range after being suppresed. If enemies continue to be in range of energy, they will be supressed until the energy is gone. Instead of dying, when an opponent deals enough damage to kill an ally, KAY/O gets knocked down if he is still in his ultimate and can get ressurected by a teammate by stabilizing KAY/O's core.
