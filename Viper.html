Role

Viper's role is Controller

Abilities

Viper's abilities lets her cut off vision of enemies, which lets her and her teammates get onto site with ease.

Ability Default Key Uses/Restock Credit/Points
Snake Bite C 2 200
Poison Cloud Q 1 200
Toxic Screen E 1 Free
Viper's Pit X N/A 7 Ultimate Points

Snake Bite

Press C to equip a chemical launcher. Fire (left-click) to launch the chemical that shatters upon hitting the ground, creating a chemical zone that damages enemies and allies.

Toxic Screen

Press E to equip a gas emitter launcher. Fire (left-click) to deploy a long line of gas emitters. Press E when the gas emitters are deployed to create a tall wall of toxic gas that cost fuel to use. This ability can be used more than once.

Poison Cloud

Press Q to equip a gas emitter. Fire (left-click) to throw the emitter and it can be picked up when near it. Press Q when the gas emitter is thrown to create a toxic gas in a shape of a sphere. This ability can be used more than once.

Viper's Pit

Press X to equip a chemical sprayer. Fire (left-click) to spray a chemical cloud around Viper that takes up a big amount of space. Enemies that go into this chemical cloud take decay damage and gets their vision reduced.
