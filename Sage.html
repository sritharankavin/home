Sage's role is Sentinel.

Abilities

Sage's abilties comes from her Earthbending; a special power she gained from “The First Light”.

Barrier Orb

Press C to equip a Barrier Orb. Fire (left-click) to place a wall. Alternative fire (right-click) rotates the wall.

Healing Orb

Press E to equip a healing orb. Fire (left-click) with your crosshair hovering over an ally that is damaged to heal them over–time. Alternative fire (right-click) while Sage is damaged to heal herself over–time.

Slow Orb

Press Q to equip a slow orb. Fire (left-click) to throw the slow orb forward that breaks upon landing, creating a field that slows down players.

Ressurection

Press X to equip a ressurrection ability. Fire (left-click) with your crosshair hovering over a dead ally to ressurect them. After a set amount of time, the ally will be brought back to life with full health (no shield).
