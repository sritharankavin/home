Role

Breach's role is Initiator

Abilities

Like all Agents in Valorant, Breach has 4 abilities. This agent is decently hard to play but there are a couple of agents that are much harder to play.

Flash Point

Breach’s first ability is Flash Point. Press Q to equip a blinding charge. Fire (left-click unless you changed the fire bind to a different key) the charge to set a fast-acting burst through the wall blinding whoever is looking at it (this blinds teammates if they look at it).


Fault line

Breach’s second ability is Flash Point. Press E to equip a seismic blast. Hold Fire to increase the distance and then release to set of a tremor, stunning all opponents and allies in its range.


Aftershock

Breach’s 3rd ability is Aftershock. Press c to equip a fusion charge. Fire the charge to set a slow-acting burst through the wall. This burst does high damage to anyone caught in its area.


Rolling Thunder

Breach’s final ability also known as ultimate is Rolling Thunder. Equip a seismic charge. Fire to send a cascading tremor in 1 large area. The tremor knocks up and stuns anyone caught in it.
