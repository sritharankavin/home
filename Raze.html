Role

Raze's role is Duelist.

Abilities

Raze has 4 abilities that can help her team and herself get onto site and take control of it.

Ability Default Key Uses/Restock Credit/Points
Boom Bot C 1 300
Blast Pack Q 2 200
Paint Shells E 1 2 kills
Showstopper X N/A 8 Ultimate Points

Boom Bot

Press C to equip a Boom Bot. Fire (left-click) to deploy the bot. The bot travels in a straight line on the ground and bounces off the walls. The Boom Bot will lock onto any enemies in its vision and chase them. When it reaches the enemy, it will explode, dealing heavy damage to the enemy.

Paint Shells

Press E to equip a cluster grenade. Fire (left-click) to throw the cluster grenade, which creates smaller grenades after it explodes, dealing high damage to anyone in the range.

Blast Pack

Press Q to throw a Blast Pack that sticks to surfaces. When the Blast Pack is thrown, Press Q to detonate it, dealing damage and moving anything hit. Raze can't get damaged by this ability, but she will take fall damage if she is launched up high enough.

Showstopper

Press X to equip a rocket launcher. Fire (left-click) shoots a rocket that does massive damage to anyone within the area it hits.
