		<p> Press C to equip an owl drone. Fire (left-click) to deploy the drone and be in control of the movement of the drone. When in control of the drone, fire (left-click) to shoot a marking dart. This dart will reveal the location of an enemy who is struck by it. </p>

		<p> Press E to equip a bow with a recon bolt. Fire (left-click) to send the recon bolt forward, sticking to the surface it lands on and catching the enemies caught in the line of sight of the bolt. Hold fire (left-click) to extend the range of the bolt. Alternative fire (right-click) to add 1 or 2 bounces to the arrow. </p>

		<h2 id = "f"> Hunter's Fury </h2>
		<p> Press X to equip a bow that will shoot three long-range energy blasts that go through the walls. Fire (left-click) to release an energy blast in a line in front of Sova, dealing high damage and revealing the location of enemies caught in the line. You can shoot the energy blast two more times while the ability timer is active. </p>
