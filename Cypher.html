Cypher's role is Sentinel.

Abilities

Cypher is one of the few agents that have no special abilities at all.

Cyber Cage

Press Q to toss the cyber cage in front of Cypher. Press F to activate it which creates a zone that blocks vision. This also makes a noise when an enemy goes in the zone.


Spycam

Press E to equip a spycam. Fire (left-click) at the targeted location if it's in range to place the spycam. When the spycam is placed, you can press E to take control of the camera's view. When in control of the camera, fire (left-click) to shoot a marking dart. If the marking dart is shot at an enemy, it will reveal their location until they remove it.


Trapwire

Press C to equip a trapwire. Fire (left-click) to place a trapwire at the targeted location creating a line that spans between the placed location and the wall opposite of it. When an enemy is near the trapwire, they will be able to see it and can destroy it, but if the enemy crosses through the tripwire, they will be revealed and dazed unless they destroy the device in time. This will also alert the character if an enemy is caught by a trapwire or destroy a trapwire.


Neural Theft

After an enemy dies, within 20 seconds you can press X on them to reveal the location of all the enemies that are alive.
