Role

Yoru's role is Duelist

Abilities

Yoru's abilities let him get onto site while taking out opponents one by one without them knowing where he could be next.

Fake Out

Press C to equip a decoy of Yoru. Fire (left-click) to activate the decoy and send it forward. Alt fire (right-click) to place an inactive decoy. Press E over the inactive decoy to make it become active and send it forward. When the decoy is shot at, it will explode into a blinding flash, blinding anyone who sees it.

Gate Crash

Press E to equip a rift tether. Fire (left-click) to send the rift tether forward. Alt fire (right-click) to place a rift theter that is stationary. Press E to activate the rift tether, making Yoru teleport to where the rift tether is. Press E while hovering over the rift tether to trigger a fake teleport.

Blindside

Press Q to equip an unstable dimensional fragment from reality. Fire (left-click) to throw the dimensional fragment, activating a flash that winds up once it collides with a surface.

Dimensional Drift

Press X to equip a mask that can see between dimensions. Fire (left-click) to drift into Yoru's dimension, allowing Yoru to move at great speed and unable to be affected or seen by enemies from the other dimension.
