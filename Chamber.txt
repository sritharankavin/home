		<h2 id = "c"> Headhunter </h2>
		<p> Press Q to equip a heavy pistol. Alt Fire (right-click) to aim down sights with the pistol and Fire (left-click) to shoot the pistol. </p>

		<h2 id = "d"> Rendezvous </h2>
		<p> Press E and Fire (left-click) to place down a teleporter. Place another teleporter that is in range and press E to teleport back to the other teleport. Works vice versa and the teleporters can be picked up, but will have a cooldown to place them back down. </p>

		<h2 id = "e"> Trademark </h2>
		<p> Press C to equip a trap. Fire to place down the trap. When visible enemies come in range, the trap counts down and then makes a slow field which players will be slowed if in it. If enemies get noticed or slowed by the trap, it will alert the character. </p>

		<h2 id = "f"> Tour De Force </h2>
		<p> Press X to summons a sniper that can kill an enemy with a direct hit. When an enemy is killed by this weapon, it creates a field that slows players caught inside of it. The sniper has only 5 bullets. </p>
