Role

Neon's role is Duelist

Abilities

Neon's has 4 abilities that can be used to lead the team onto site and get early picks.

Ability Default Key Uses/Restock Credit/Points
Fast Lane C 1 300
Relay Bolt Q 2 200
High Gear E 1 (Use) 2 kills (restock) Free
Overdrive X N/A 7 Ultimate Points

Fast Lane

Press C to send two energy lines forward that travels in a straight line, stopping if it comes in contact with any terrain. The walls block vision and deals damage to enemies passing through them.

High Gear

Press E to channel Neon's energy into increased speed. Alt Fire (right-click) to do a electric slide. The slide charge resets every two kills.

Relay Bolt

Press Q to throw an energy bolt that bounces once. When the bolt hits the terrain, it electrifies the ground below, creating a concussive blast.

Overdrive

Press X to unleash Neon's full power and speed for a short amount of time. Fire (left-click) to channel Neon's energy into a deadly lightning beam with high movement accuracy. The time that Neon's full power lasts resets on each kill.
