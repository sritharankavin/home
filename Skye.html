Role

Skye's role is Initiator.

Abilities

Skye's abilities lets her clear corners and catch enemies off guard.

Ability Default Key Uses/Restock Credit/Points

Regrowth

Press C to equip a healing trinket. Hold fire (left-click) to heal allies in range of the healing trinket. The healing trinket can be reused until the healing bar is depleted, and Skye cannot heal herself.

Guiding Light

Press E to equip a hawk trinket. Fire (left-click) to send the hawk trinket forward. Hold fire (left-click) to guide the hawk trinket in the direction of your crosshair. Press E again while the hawk is in flight to turn it into a flash that makes a noise if an enemy was blinded.

Trailblazer

Press Q to equip a tiger trinket. Fire (left-click) to send and take control of the tiger. When in control of the tiger, fire (left-click) to leap forward, exploding in a concussive blast and damaging enemies that are directly hit.

Seekers

Press X to equip a Seeker trinket. Fire (left-click) to send out three Seekers that track down the three closest enemies. If an enemy is caught by a Seeker, it nearsights them for a few seconds.
