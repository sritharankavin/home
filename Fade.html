Role

Fade's role is Initiator.

Abilities

Like all Agents in Valorant, Fade has 4 Abilities. Her utility is used to gain information on the enemies of their location.

Ability Default Key Uses/Restock Credit/Points
Prowler C 2 250
Seize Q 1 200
Haunt E 1 (Use) 40 seconds (Restock) Free
Nightfall X N/A 7 Ultimate Points

Seize

Press Q to equip an sphere of nightmare ink. Fire (left-click) to throw the sphere which will hit the ground after a set amount of time. Upon hitting the ground, the ink will explode and create a zone which enemies who are caught in it cannot escape the zone. When the sphere of nightmare ink is in the air, you can press Q again to make it plummet to the ground.


Haunt

Press E to equip a nightmarish entity. Fire (left-click) to throw the nightmarish entity orb which will hit the ground after a set amount of time. Upon hitting the ground, the nightmarish entity will reveal the location of enemies caught in its line of sight. Enemies can destroy this entity, but if they don't, it will disappear after a set amount of time. Press E again when the entity is thrown into the air to make it plummet the ground before the set amount of time.


Prowler

Press C to equip a Prowler. Fire (left-click) to send the Prowler out, causing it to travel in a straight line. If an enemy comes in vision of the Prowler, it will lock onto the enemy and chase them, and if it catches the enemy, the enemy will get nearsighted for a few seconds. Holding the fire (left-click) button to control the Prowler by going in the direction of your crosshair.


Nightfall

Press X to equip the power of Fear. Fire (left-click) to send out a wave of nightmare energy that can go through walls. Anyone caught in this will be deafened, decayed, and will have a trail of ink that reveals their location.
