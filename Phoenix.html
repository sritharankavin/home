Role

Phoenix's role is Duelist.

Abilities

Phoenix has 4 abilities that he can use to help him and his teammates get onto site and let him engage in early battles.

Ability Default Key Uses/Restock Credit/Points
Blaze C 1 200
Curveball Q 2 250
Hot Hands E 1 2 kills
Run it Back X N/A 6 Ultimate Points

Blaze

Press C to equip a flame wall. Fire (left-click) to release the wall, creating a line of flame that movse forward. Enemies and teammates passing by the fire gets damaged. Hold fire (left-click) to bend the wall in the direction of your crosshair. You can use this wall to heal Phoenix.

Hot Hands

Press E to equip a fireball. Fire (left-click) to throw a fireball that explodes after a set amount of time or upon hitting the ground, creating a fire zone that damages players and heals Phoenix.

Curveball

Press Q to equip a fire orb that curves and detonates after throwing. Fire (left-click) to curve the fire orb to the left, detonating after a set amount of time and blinding any player who sees the orb. Alternative fire (right-click) to curve the fire orb to the right.

Run it Back

Press X to instantly place a marker at Phoenix's location. When this ability is active, and Phoenix dies or the timer expires will end this ability and bring Phoenix back to his location where the marker was set with full health.
